Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 1, 2026

Your privacy is a commitment, not a checkbox. This policy explains clearly and honestly how Bzillion.Club collects, uses, and protects your personal information as an ELITE member.

01 Information We Collect

We collect information you provide when interacting with our platform:

  • Account registration — name, email address, username
  • Purchase and transaction data processed via WarriorPlus
  • USDC wallet address and network details for commission payouts
  • Auto Mailer and Bulk Mailer content you create and schedule
  • Broadcast content, messages, and platform activity
  • Support communications sent to admin@bzillion.club
  • IP address, browser type, device data, and usage analytics

02 How We Use Your Information

  • Provide and maintain your ELITE membership and all associated tools
  • Process your WarriorPlus purchase and activate your account instantly
  • Deliver weekly USDC commission payouts every Sunday
  • Operate the Auto Mailer and Bulk Mailer on your behalf to your referral list
  • Assign free referrals to your account as an ELITE benefit
  • Send transactional emails — confirmations, payout alerts, account notices
  • Assign and support your dedicated relationship manager
  • Detect and prevent fraud, abuse, and VPN circumvention
  • Comply with applicable legal obligations under Indian law

03 We Do NOT Sell Your Data

We do not sell, rent, trade, or transfer your personal information to any third party for their marketing purposes. Your data is used exclusively to operate your ELITE membership. We are fully CAN-SPAM compliant.

04 Auto Mailer & Bulk Mailer Data

Email content you create in the Auto Mailer and Bulk Mailer is stored securely solely to enable automated delivery to your referral list. This content is:

  • Used exclusively to send emails on your behalf to your own referrals
  • Never shared with other members or third parties
  • Deleted when you remove it from your dashboard

05 USDC Wallet & Financial Data

Your USDC wallet address is stored solely to process your commission payouts. This information is encrypted and never shared outside the payment processing requirement. All payment card processing is handled entirely by WarriorPlus — Bzillion.Club does not store card or banking data.

06 Cookies

We use cookies to maintain your login session, track platform activity, and improve user experience. You may disable cookies in your browser, but some features — including your dashboard, Auto Mailer, and Bulk Mailer — may not function correctly without them.

07 Third-Party Services

  • WarriorPlus — Payment processing and affiliate management. Subject to their own privacy policy.
  • Cloudflare — CDN and security. Your IP may be processed through their global network.
  • Trustpilot — Independent review platform. Governed by Trustpilot's own privacy terms.

08 Data Security

We implement industry-standard technical and organizational measures to protect your data — including encrypted storage, secure HTTPS connections, and restricted internal access. No Internet transmission is 100% secure, but we take every reasonable precaution.

09 Your Rights

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Request deletion of your personal data (subject to legal retention requirements)
  • Opt out of marketing communications via unsubscribe links at any time
  • Deactivate auto-renewal or close your account via Profile → Settings

10 Children's Privacy

Bzillion.Club is intended for users 18 years of age and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors. If you believe a minor has registered, contact us immediately at admin@bzillion.club.
